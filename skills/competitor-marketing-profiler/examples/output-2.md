## Section 5 — Strategic Takeaways

**🟢 Pursue**
- **Build consideration-stage content for buyers comparing video tools.** Vidyard's content creates awareness that video matters in sales but almost nothing for the buyer now evaluating specific tools — workflow comparison guides, use-case specifics, and implementation content for common sales motions are wide open and rank for high-intent queries.
- **Make the bottom-up sales motion explicit.** Content and product flows built for the individual rep who wants to test video prospecting without a procurement process — one closed deal becomes internal advocacy, and that story directly counters Vidyard's org-first positioning.
- **Build a presence where younger sales professionals discover tools.** TikTok and the sales communities Vidyard ignores are early-mover territory — establishing a voice there now compounds before Vidyard notices the gap.

**🛡️ Defend**
- **Don't try to out-ROI Vidyard in messaging.** Their pipeline and revenue outcome claims are their strongest asset and their audience is primed to believe them — competing on the same proof points without equivalent sales-specific data reinforces their authority rather than challenging it. Lead with speed, adoption simplicity, and cross-functional value instead.
- **Protect the casual, accessible brand voice.** It's a genuine differentiator for bottom-up adoption — tightening it too much in pursuit of enterprise credibility risks losing the thing that makes individual contributors want to champion Loom internally.

**👁️ Watch**
- **Vidyard expanding into async communication broadly.** Right now they're focused on sales video specifically, but if they move toward general async messaging they enter Loom's widest market directly — with a stronger sales-professional user base to cross-sell from.
- **LinkedIn algorithm shift toward more organic reach.** Vidyard has built significant authority there; any shift that amplifies that channel would widen the gap in B2B reach at a moment when Loom's LinkedIn presence isn't yet competitive.
